About Ooty

Nestled in the Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, Ooty (officially known as Udhagamandalam) stands as one of India's most beloved hill stations. At an elevation of 2,240 meters above sea level, this charming destination offers a perfect escape from the scorching heat of the plains.

Perfect Climate

Pleasant weather throughout the year with temperatures ranging from 15°C to 25°C

Top Attractions

Ooty Lake

Artificial lake with boating facilities, surrounded by eucalyptus trees and beautiful walking paths

Toy Train

UNESCO World Heritage narrow-gauge railway offering breathtaking mountain views

Botanical Gardens

55 acres of diverse plants, flowers, and trees with annual flower shows

Doddabetta Peak

Highest point in Nilgiris offering panoramic views and telescope house

Rose Garden

Over 20,000 varieties of roses spread across 10 acres

Tea Gardens

Sprawling tea plantations with guided tours and fresh tea tasting

Best Time to Visit

Summer (Apr-Jun)

Perfect weather, ideal for sightseeing but expect crowds

Monsoon (Jul-Sep)

Lush green landscapes, perfect for nature lovers

Winter (Oct-Mar)

Cool weather, misty mornings, perfect for cozy stays
