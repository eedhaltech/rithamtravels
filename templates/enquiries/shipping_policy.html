Service-Based Business Notice

This is a service-based travel business. No physical products are shipped. All bookings and confirmations are delivered digitally.

1. Nature of Our Business

Ritham Tours & Travels is a service-based travel and tourism company. We provide:

  • Transportation services (cab rentals, tour vehicles)
  • Travel planning and consultation services
  • Tour package bookings and arrangements
  • Travel-related advisory services

We do not manufacture, sell, or ship any physical products.

2. Digital Delivery of Services

2.1 Booking Confirmations

  • Email Delivery: Booking confirmations sent to registered email address
  • SMS Notifications: Booking details and updates via SMS
  • WhatsApp Messages: Trip details and driver information
  • Instant Delivery: All confirmations delivered within minutes of booking

2.2 Travel Documents

  • E-tickets and vouchers delivered via email
  • Itinerary details shared digitally
  • Driver and vehicle information sent before trip
  • Emergency contact details provided electronically

2.3 Payment Receipts

  • Digital receipts generated automatically
  • Tax invoices sent via email
  • Payment confirmations through Razorpay
  • Refund receipts (if applicable) delivered digitally

3. Service Delivery Method

3.1 Transportation Services

  • Pickup Location: Services delivered at customer's specified location
  • On-Time Service: Vehicles arrive at scheduled time
  • Professional Drivers: Trained and licensed drivers provided
  • Clean Vehicles: Well-maintained and sanitized vehicles

3.2 Service Areas

  • Primary Service Area: Coimbatore and surrounding areas
  • Outstation Services: Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka
  • Local Services: Within city limits and nearby attractions
  • Airport Transfers: To/from major airports in the region

5. Contact Information Accuracy

5.1 Customer Responsibility

  • Provide accurate email address for digital delivery
  • Ensure mobile number is active and reachable
  • Check spam/junk folders for important communications
  • Update contact information if changed after booking

5.2 Delivery Failure

  • If email delivery fails, we will attempt SMS delivery
  • If SMS fails, we will call the registered mobile number
  • Customers can always access booking details through our website
  • Alternative contact methods available for urgent communications

6. Data Security and Privacy

  • All digital communications are encrypted and secure
  • Personal information protected as per our Privacy Policy
  • Booking details accessible only to authorized personnel
  • Customer data not shared with third parties without consent

7. Service Modifications and Updates

  • Emergency notifications delivered instantly
  • Service changes confirmed through multiple channels
