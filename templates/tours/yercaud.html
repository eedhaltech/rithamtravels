About Yercaud

Nestled in the Shevaroy Hills of Tamil Nadu at 1,515 meters above sea level, Yercaud is a charming hill station known for its coffee plantations, orange groves, and pleasant climate. Often called the "Jewel of South," it offers a perfect retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Coffee & Orange Paradise

Famous for its aromatic coffee plantations and sweet orange groves spread across the hills

Top Attractions

Yercaud Lake

Scenic artificial lake perfect for boating and peaceful walks around its perimeter

Lady's Seat

Popular viewpoint offering panoramic views of Salem and surrounding plains

Pagoda Point

Highest point in Yercaud with breathtaking views and ancient Rama temple

Botanical Garden

Beautiful garden with rare orchids, roses, and diverse plant species

Kiliyur Falls

Spectacular waterfall cascading from 300 feet height during monsoon season

Coffee Plantations

Aromatic coffee estates offering guided tours and fresh coffee tasting

Best Time to Visit

Summer (Mar-May)

Pleasant weather perfect for sightseeing and outdoor activities

Monsoon (Jun-Sep)

Lush green landscapes and waterfalls at their spectacular best

Winter (Oct-Feb)

Cool and misty weather, ideal for coffee plantation tours and lake activities
