About Mysore

Known as the cultural capital of Karnataka, Mysore is a city steeped in royal heritage and architectural grandeur. Famous for its magnificent palaces, vibrant festivals, and traditional arts, it offers a perfect blend of history and modernity.

Royal Heritage City

Former capital of the Kingdom of Mysore, home to the magnificent Mysore Palace and royal traditions

Top Attractions

Mysore Palace

Magnificent Indo-Saracenic palace illuminated with 97,000 bulbs during Dussehra festival

Chamundi Hills

Sacred hill with Chamundeshwari Temple and panoramic views of Mysore city

Brindavan Gardens

Beautiful terraced gardens with musical fountain shows and colorful illumination

Devaraja Market

Vibrant traditional market famous for sandalwood, silk, spices, and jasmine flowers

Jaganmohan Palace

Art gallery and museum housing one of India's largest collections of artifacts

Mysore Zoo

One of India's oldest and most well-maintained zoos with diverse wildlife species

Best Time to Visit

Summer (Mar-May)

Warm weather, ideal for palace visits and indoor attractions

Monsoon (Jun-Sep)

Pleasant weather with lush gardens and beautiful fountain shows

Winter (Oct-Feb)

Perfect weather and Dussehra festival season - the best time to visit
