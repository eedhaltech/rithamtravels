About Ritham Tours & Travels

Your trusted travel partner for over a decade, creating unforgettable journeys across India with comfort, safety, and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Ritham Tours & Travels began as a small family business with a simple vision: to make travel accessible, comfortable, and memorable for everyone. What started with a single vehicle and a dream has grown into one of South India's most trusted travel companies.

Based in the beautiful city of Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, we have been serving travelers from across India and beyond. Our journey has been marked by countless smiles, satisfied customers, and the joy of being part of their special moments.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of well-maintained vehicles and employ experienced drivers who share our commitment to safety and service excellence. Every journey with us is crafted with care, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of what makes travel truly special.
